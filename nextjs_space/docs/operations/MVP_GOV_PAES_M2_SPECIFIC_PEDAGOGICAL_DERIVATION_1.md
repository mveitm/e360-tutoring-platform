## Why BPTT is not duplicated for M2

BPTT is the transversal tutoring grammar for Bexauri. Duplicating it as a separate "BPTT de M2" would fragment the canonical pedagogical layer and create competing authorities.

PAES_M2 instead requires a specific derivation from BPTT: the BPTT grammar remains transversal, and the PAES_M2 branch documents how that grammar is expressed in a test-oriented mathematics M2 tutoring domain.

## Scope correction: MVP cerrado / Sales-Ready path

MVP cerrado for M1/M2/L1 must not be described as merely limited/minimal. It is the closed validation scope that verifies whether the platform has everything required to move toward MVP Sales-Ready.

This does not mean full PAES universe coverage or public Sales-Ready claims. It means the closed MVP must be complete enough to validate the intended Sales-Ready product promise across M1/M2/L1.

Seeds and first capsules are implementation steps, not the final definition of closed MVP scope.

## Relationship to Program PAES_M2

This phase does not create or align `Program PAES_M2`.

It documents the branch that must exist before PAES_M2 is aligned as a local/dev Program. Program alignment remains a later technical/data phase and must not be collapsed into this governance derivation.
