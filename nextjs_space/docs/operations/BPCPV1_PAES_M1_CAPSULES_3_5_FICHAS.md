## 7. Ficha capsule 3 — pedagogical purpose

Observe whether the student can identify a base quantity, coordinate a simple proportional relation, calculate carefully, and interpret whether the result makes sense in context.

Capsule 3 provides a standalone Numbers/proportionality signal after initial Algebra/functions contact. It helps prevent early M1 from narrowing too quickly into AS1 and gives evidence about base quantity, percentage, unit-rate comparison, and ratio/equivalence.

## 8. Ficha capsule 3 — non-purpose

- a diagnosis of Numbers mastery;
- a remedial route;
- a PAES score/readiness signal;
- adaptive AI;
- a personalized route decision;
- a proof of proportional reasoning mastery;
- a basis for strong student-level claims.

## 11. Ficha capsule 3 — likely error/distractor families

Likely families:

- treating the percent number as an absolute amount;
- using the wrong base quantity;
- reporting the decrease instead of the remaining amount;
- comparing total prices rather than unit prices;
- additive reasoning where proportional scaling is needed;
- multiplying visible values without preserving relation;
- arithmetic slip that should not be overread as conceptual failure.
