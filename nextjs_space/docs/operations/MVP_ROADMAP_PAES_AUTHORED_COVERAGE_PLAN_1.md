## Authored coverage principles

Coverage is not only a capsule count. Each authored coverage unit must satisfy:

- visible stable correlative;
- clear title and contentKey;
- M1/M2 item count maximum 4, L1 answerable-question range 1-4;
- brief and complete authoredFeedback when applicable;
- coherent stems/options/correctOptionKey;
- no internal leakage;
- required table/graph/figure/image/math/text-base stimulus present and reviewable;
- deterministic renderer or approved asset for final visual stimulus;
- human/static QA evidence before pilot claims;
- mobile-readable feedback and visual stimuli;
- continuity implications documented separately from capsule quality.

Coverage must also span:

- multiple PAES skills, not only repeated procedural algebra;
- different axes/topics inside each program;
- mixed difficulty within a safe pilot range;
- formats including text-only, tables, graph/figure-ready items, and L1 text-base items where appropriate.

## M1 coverage recommendation

Recommended M1 coverage:

| hito | recommended authored threshold | rationale |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| minimum circuit validation | 8 smoke-passed capsules | already reached for current repaired M1 route-order set, within local/dev scope |
| closed pilot minimum | 12-16 authored capsules | enough to show early teaching/calibration breadth without implying full M1 preparation |
| before productive agentic | 24-32 curated authored capsules plus metadata/QA | enough for recommendation/assembly experiments without overfitting to a tiny bank |

M1 closed-pilot coverage should not be more of the same algebra/function sequence only. It should include:

- current repaired M1-C01 through M1-C08;
- additional coverage across numbers/proportionality, geometry, probability/statistics, algebra/functions;
- at least one repaired geometry/figure stimulus path before geometry enters the active pilot route;
- table/graph stimulus compliance for any data representation item;
- regression smoke after each batch.

M1 should not be extended blindly beyond M1-C08 until the next authored scope identifies which PAES axes/skills are being added and which visual-stimulus/rendering obligations they create.

## Thresholds before agentic

Productive agentic remains blocked until a curated authored bank and QA workflow exist.

Minimum before agentic recommendation:

- M1: 24-32 authored/curated capsules with stable metadata, axis/skill tags, visual stimulus status, feedback status, smoke status, and continuity meaning.
- M2: 12-20 authored/curated capsules if agentic may recommend M2 content.
- L1: 12-20 authored/curated text-base capsules if agentic may recommend L1 content.
- All: no unresolved P0 feedback, key, leakage, visual-stimulus, or continuity blockers in the recommendation pool.

Minimum before agentic generation:

- Not authorized by this plan.
- Requires a separate governance phase, QA gates, deterministic rendering rules, human approval workflow, and explicit product boundary.
