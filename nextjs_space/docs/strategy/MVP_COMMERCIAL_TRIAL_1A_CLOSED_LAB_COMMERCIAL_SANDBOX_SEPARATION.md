## 3. Core strategic thesis

Bexauri may test real payment before public Sales-Ready only inside a closed, controlled, explicitly non-public commercial sandbox.

The purpose is to validate commercial infrastructure and coherence between payment, access, continuity, expiry, and basic support.

That test would not validate a public offer, public price, market demand, or Sales-Ready status.

Public Sales-Ready remains blocked until Bexauri can offer minimally effective PAES support across M1, M2, and L1.

## 4. Separation rules

* Closed commercial sandbox is not public launch.
* Closed payment test is not public sale.
* Payment/access continuity test is not market validation.
* M1-only closed-lab work is not public M1/M2/L1 Sales-Ready.
* Limited-scope subscription concept is not a complete PAES platform.
* `StudentAccess` state is not a commercial promise.
* `reaffirm_no_access` endpoint is not access activation.
* Human supervision is not manual tutoring dependency.

## 5. Two commercial thresholds

### Level A - Closed Commercial Sandbox

Purpose:

* Test payment, access, continuity, expiry, and support coherence.

Users:

* Known controlled participants only, such as Mauricio, his son, and his son's friend, subject to high-level review when minors or payment are involved.

Nature:

* Closed.
* Controlled.
* Non-public.
* Experimental.

Allowed at this level:

* Strategy documentation.
* Risk matrix.
* Invitation/activation criteria.
* Runbook.
* Copy candidates.
* Payment flow readiness.
* Possible controlled payment later only after explicit gated authorization.

Not allowed at this level:

* Public launch.
* Sales-Ready claim.
* Broad marketing.
* Complete PAES promise.
* Uncontrolled external students.

### Level B - Public Sales-Ready MVP

Purpose:

* Public initial sale.

Minimum condition:

* Minimally effective PAES support for M1, M2, and L1.

Not validated by Level A alone:

* Public price.
* Market demand.
* Product maturity.
* Long-term subscription viability.
* Full commercial claim.

## 6. Commercial principles

* Closed payment is not public sale.
* The student is the center of the experience, but payment with minors requires high-level/Pro review.
* The app must sustain the experience; the human role supervises and audits.
* Do not sell smoke.
* Do not promise scores.
* Do not use three known users as market validation.
* Do not declare Sales-Ready because payment works.
* Do not activate access if the minimum pedagogical experience is not ready.
* Do not convert subscription into an outcome promise.

Scope:

## 8. What closed payment can validate

* Payment flow.
* Payment confirmation.
* Access transition readiness.
* Expiry behavior.
* Continuity after payment.
* Support path.
* Operational coherence.
* Mismatch between payment, access, and learning flow.
* Whether the app can sustain a basic experience without constant human intervention.

## 9. What closed payment cannot validate

* Sales-Ready public status.
* Public pricing.
* Market demand.
* Brand readiness.
* Complete PAES readiness.
* M2/L1 readiness.
* Long-term retention.
* Large-scale support load.
* Pedagogical quality at scale.

## 10. Relationship to StudentAccess

* Current `StudentAccess` implementation supports `no_access` default and admin `reaffirm_no_access`.
* It does not activate trial.
* It does not activate runtime.
* It does not activate billing.
* It does not expose student-facing access state.
* Future trial/payment/access transitions require separate phases.
* Closed commercial sandbox strategy must not bypass `StudentAccess` governance.

## 12. No-overpromise language

Allowed language:

* Closed laboratory.
* Controlled test.
* Pedagogical support in development.
* Learning trajectories.
* Logical study loads.
* Strengthening PAES competencies.
* Supervised accompaniment.

Forbidden language:

* Guaranteed PAES.
* Raise your score.
* Complete AI tutor.
* Total personalization.
* Definitive PAES platform.
* Complete preparation.
* Advanced intelligent automation.
* Learn without effort.

Guide phrase:

Bexauri seeks to help students strengthen PAES competencies through a guided, progressive, and supervised study experience, without promising results it cannot yet prove.
