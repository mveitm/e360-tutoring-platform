## 10. Answerability and ambiguity review

Review result: low answerability and ambiguity risk for locating-information use, pending future item review.

Strengths:

* Details are concrete.
* Chronology is simple.
* Conditions are explicit.
* Most references have clear antecedents.
* The future correct answer for locating-information tasks should be defensible if item wording remains narrow.

Risks:

* Future answer alternatives must avoid using near-duplicate detail wording that creates accidental ambiguity.

No current ambiguity requires blocking the draft.

## 11. Cognitive-load and difficulty review

Review result: controlled for early locating-information pilot review.

The draft is approximately 143 words, which fits the short target band. It has a concrete sequence and mostly familiar objects. Cognitive load is controlled because the student does not need specialized knowledge, real-world institutional knowledge, or current-event context.
