## 4. Allowed and disallowed language

| Category | Allowed language | Disallowed language | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Market frame | Closed laboratory | Launch | Prevents public Sales-Ready implication. |
| Commercial frame | Controlled test | Public sale | Keeps the sandbox distinct from commercial launch. |
| Pedagogical promise | Pedagogical support in development | Complete preparation | Avoids overpromising M1/M2/L1 coverage. |
| Study model | Learning trajectories / logical study loads | Complete AI tutor | Avoids unsupported automation claims. |
| Outcome | Strengthen PAES competencies | Guaranteed score increase | Avoids unverifiable outcome promises. |
| Scope | Limited scope | Total access | Keeps expectations aligned to closed-lab readiness. |
| Support | Supervised | Unlimited human support | Avoids unsustainable support commitments. |
| Continuity | Future continuity inside defined scope | Complete subscription | Avoids mature product/subscription implication. |

## 5. Core framing statement

Guide phrase:

Bexauri seeks to help students strengthen PAES competencies through a guided, progressive, and supervised study experience, without promising results it cannot yet prove.

Sober variants:

* Bexauri is testing a closed learning experience to observe whether its guided study flow is clear, useful, and sustainable for students.
* This closed laboratory is meant to learn from real use in a limited scope, not to present Bexauri as a complete PAES preparation product.
* Bexauri is developing a supervised study experience for PAES skills, with careful limits on what is available and what can be promised today.

We are inviting a small closed group to try Bexauri in a controlled learning laboratory.

This is not a public launch, not a public sale, and not complete PAES preparation. The scope is limited. The goal is to observe whether the study flow is clear, whether the student can continue with basic autonomy, and where the experience creates value or friction.

We would like to invite you to participate in a small closed Bexauri laboratory.

This is a limited and supervised test. It is not a public offer, not complete PAES preparation, and it does not promise score results. Participation in the invitation stage does not mean payment or automatic access. Any future start of the experience would be reviewed separately.

### Version C - Formal/operational

Bexauri is preparing a closed, controlled, non-public learning laboratory with a limited number of known participants.

The purpose is to evaluate clarity, continuity, and operational value in a limited study scope. This laboratory is not a commercial launch, does not represent public Sales-Ready status, and does not claim complete PAES M1/M2/L1 coverage.

Invitation status does not authorize payment, activation, trial access, runtime access, subscription, or any public offer. Any activation would require a later explicit review and phase.

## 9. Expiry / pause / closure copy candidates

### Normal sandbox closure

This closed Bexauri laboratory stage has reached its planned closure point. Thank you for helping us observe the experience. We will review what worked, what created friction, and what should improve before any future continuity decision.

### Operational pause

We are pausing this closed-lab experience for operational review. This pause is part of the controlled test and is not the student's fault. We will review the issue before deciding whether and how the experience can continue.

### Technical issue

We detected a technical issue that affects the closed-lab experience. We are pausing or limiting the activity while we review it. We will not treat this as a student failure, and we will not promise automatic continuity until the issue is understood.

### Continuity not decided

The current closed-lab scope is complete. We have not made a decision about future continuity or subscription. Any next step would require a separate review and clear scope.
