## 7. Final refined internal-use copy

The following copy is approved only as an internal future-use artifact. It is not approved for sending in this phase.

```text
Hola [Nombre],

Queremos invitarte a considerar una participacion en un laboratorio cerrado de Bexauri.

Bexauri esta en desarrollo. En esta etapa queremos observar, con mucho cuidado y en un grupo pequeno, si la experiencia de estudio guiado es clara, util y sostenible: si se entiende el primer paso, si las actividades orientan bien, y donde aparecen dudas, fricciones o necesidades de mejora.

Esto no es un lanzamiento publico, no es una venta publica y no es una preparacion PAES completa. El alcance es limitado y supervisado. Tampoco hay promesa de resultados ni de puntaje.

Esta invitacion no significa pago, acceso automatico, activacion de una experiencia, trial, suscripcion ni compromiso de continuidad. Si despues de revisar el alcance corresponde avanzar, cualquier inicio tendria que definirse en un paso futuro, revisado y explicito.

La idea, por ahora, es confirmar si este laboratorio cerrado podria tener sentido para ti dentro de limites claros y sin sobrepromesas.
```

## 8. Why this copy was selected

This copy is safer than the alternatives because it:

* Keeps "laboratorio cerrado" as the main frame.
* Explains the development state plainly.
* States the purpose as observation of clarity, usefulness, sustainability, doubts, friction, and improvement needs.
* Explicitly rejects public launch, public sale, and complete PAES preparation.
* Explicitly rejects score/result promises.
* Separates invitation from payment, access, activation, trial, subscription, and continuity.
* Requires a future reviewed and explicit step before any start.
* Avoids internal system terms such as `StudentAccess`.
* Avoids payment, price, subscription, and billing copy.

## 9. Why other candidates were not selected

Version A was not selected as the final base because it is safe but too compressed. It may work for a short operational note, but the first internal closed-lab invitation benefits from more context and a calmer explanation.

Version C was not selected as the final base because it is precise but too formal. It is better suited for internal/admin framing than for a future participant-facing invitation candidate.
