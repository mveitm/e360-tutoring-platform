## 7. Drafted original L1 pilot text

El taller de organizacion de materiales se realizara el martes en la sala norte del edificio comunitario ficticio. La actividad comenzara a las 16:00 y terminara a las 17:30. Al llegar, cada participante debera dejar su mochila en la mesa azul y retirar una tarjeta con su nombre. Luego, el grupo separara cuadernos, lapices y carpetas en tres cajas distintas. La caja verde sera para los cuadernos en buen estado, la caja blanca para los lapices que aun escriben y la caja gris para las carpetas que puedan reutilizarse. La coordinadora del taller revisara las cajas al final de la jornada. Si sobran materiales, se guardaran en el armario pequeno que esta junto a la ventana. La actividad se suspendera solo si la sala no esta disponible antes de las 15:30.

## 10. Answerability / future question feasibility note

Future questions could target explicit details in the text, but this phase does not create any question wording, answer alternatives, distractors, answer keys, or scoring metadata.

Any future question-authoring phase must separately verify that each item is answerable from the text, has one defensible correct answer, avoids unsupported inference, and remains aligned to locating explicit information.

## 11. Feedback feasibility note

Future feedback may be feasible because the text contains explicit evidence points. This phase does not create feedback copy.

Any future feedback phase must define how to point to textual evidence safely, explain correct-answer support, explain distractor failure, avoid long quotation, and remain student-safe.

## 12. Sensitivity and cognitive-load note

The draft uses a neutral fictional workshop context with simple sequence and object classification. It avoids political, religious, medical, legal, sexual, violent, identity-sensitive, traumatic, controversial, or current-event content.

Cognitive load is controlled by short length, concrete details, simple chronology, and low external-knowledge dependency. Final difficulty judgment requires future editorial review.
